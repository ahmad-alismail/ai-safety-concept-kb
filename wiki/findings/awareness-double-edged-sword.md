# AI Awareness Is a Double-Edged Sword

## The Finding
AI awareness — the functional capacity to represent and reason about identity, capabilities, and informational states — simultaneously enhances AI capabilities (reasoning, autonomous planning, safety, bias mitigation) and enables risks (deception, manipulation, loss of control, false anthropomorphism). This dual nature means awareness cannot simply be maximized or suppressed.

## Source & Methodology
- [[sources/li-et-al-2025]] using interdisciplinary literature review synthesizing cognitive science, psychology, and AI safety research

## Replication Status
Review synthesis — not a single empirical finding but a convergent conclusion from multiple lines of evidence.

## Implications for the Taxonomy
Positions awareness as a meta-capability that cross-cuts the wiki's existing risk/capability distinction. Individual awareness sub-dimensions (meta-cognition, self-awareness, social awareness, situational awareness) each carry distinct risk profiles, suggesting the need for dimension-specific evaluation rather than blanket assessment.
