# Joe Benton

## Profile
AI safety researcher at Anthropic. First author of "Sabotage Evaluations for Frontier Models," developing the framework for evaluating models' ability to subvert oversight and decision-making.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/benton-et-al-2024]] — led development of sabotage capability evaluation framework; designed threat models, capability thresholds, and four evaluation protocols
- [[sources/gasteiger-et-al-2025]] — co-author; training-time mitigations for alignment faking in RL

## Conceptual Framework
Frames sabotage capabilities as a distinct family from standard dangerous capabilities, requiring dedicated evaluation. Advocates for end-to-end evaluations grounded in mock deployment scenarios over narrow atomic benchmarks.
