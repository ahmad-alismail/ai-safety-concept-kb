# Teun van der Weij

## Profile
AI safety researcher; ML Alignment Theory Scholars (MATS) program participant. Led the sandbagging research project, headed the formal definition of sandbagging, and wrote most of the paper.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/van-der-weij-et-al-2024]] — lead author; proposed the formal definition of AI sandbagging and demonstrated sandbagging capabilities in frontier language models

## Conceptual Framework
Defines sandbagging as a three-condition concept (strategic + underperformance + evaluation) and distinguishes developer sandbagging from AI system sandbagging. Emphasizes the separation of sandbagging *capability* from sandbagging *tendency*.
