# Stuart Armstrong

## Profile
Researcher at the Future of Humanity Institute (FHI), University of Oxford. Originator of the utility indifference technique for addressing the shutdown problem.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/soares-et-al-2015]] (co-author)
- Armstrong (forthcoming, 2015) — "AI Motivated Value Selection" — introduced the utility indifference technique analyzed in Soares et al. (2015)

## Conceptual Framework
Developed utility indifference as a method for maintaining agent indifference between shutdown and non-shutdown outcomes. The technique uses correction terms to balance expected utility across cases, preventing agents from being incentivized to manipulate shutdown probability.
