# Peter Slattery

## Profile

Researcher at MIT FutureTech and Ready Research. Lead author of the AI Risk Repository, the first comprehensive meta-review and living database of AI risks.

## Key Contributions

- [[sources/slattery-et-al-2024]]

## Conceptual Framework

Developed dual-taxonomy approach to AI risk classification: a high-level Causal Taxonomy (Entity × Intent × Timing) and a mid-level Domain Taxonomy (7 domains, 24 subdomains). Advocates for shared understanding and coordinated risk frameworks across the field.
