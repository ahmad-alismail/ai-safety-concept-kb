# Machine Intelligence Research Institute (MIRI)

## Profile
AI safety research organization focused on mathematical foundations of safe AI. Based in Berkeley, California. Supported the research behind the mesa-optimization framework.

## Key Contributions
- Supported [[sources/hubinger-et-al-2019]] — "Risks from Learned Optimization in Advanced Machine Learning Systems"
- Early conceptual work on "optimization daemons" (Yudkowsky), which the mesa-optimization framework was designed to formalize and replace
- Foundational work on corrigibility (Soares et al. 2015)

## Conceptual Framework
MIRI's research tradition emphasizes formal and theoretical analysis of AI safety risks, particularly worst-case scenarios involving deceptive or misaligned optimizers. The mesa-optimization framework exemplifies this approach: theoretical analysis of failure modes before they manifest empirically.
