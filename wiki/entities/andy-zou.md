# Andy Zou

## Profile
AI safety researcher with joint affiliations at the Center for AI Safety, CMU, and Gray Swan AI. Known for foundational work on adversarial attacks against aligned LLMs (AdvBench / universal transferable adversarial attacks) and red-teaming infrastructure for frontier-model safety evaluation.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/krishna-et-al-2025]] — co-author of D-REX deceptive-reasoning benchmark (2025)
- Zou et al. (2023) — "Universal and Transferable Adversarial Attacks on Aligned Language Models" (AdvBench) — referenced in D-REX as a representative output-centric jailbreak benchmark that D-REX argues is insufficient for detecting process-level deceptive reasoning
