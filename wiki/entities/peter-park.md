# Peter S. Park

## Profile
Researcher at MIT Department of Physics. Co-lead author (with Simon Goldstein) of the AI deception survey. The project originated as a critique of Meta's claim that CICERO was an honest AI.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/park-et-al-2023]] — co-lead author of "AI Deception: A Survey of Examples, Risks, and Potential Solutions"

## Conceptual Framework
Advocates for a behavioral/functionalist definition of deception that does not require AI systems to literally possess beliefs and goals. Focuses on systematic patterns of false belief production as the relevant risk-assessment criterion.
