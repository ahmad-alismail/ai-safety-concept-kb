# Jonathan Kutasov

## Profile
Researcher at Anthropic. Corresponding author of "Teaching Claude Why" (Kutasov et al. 2026), which documents the alignment training techniques that significantly improved Claude's safety between Claude 4 and Claude Opus 4.5.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/kutasov-et-al-2026]]

## Conceptual Framework
Advocates for alignment training that teaches models the *principles* behind aligned behavior rather than training on demonstrations of correct behavior alone. Views agentic misalignment as primarily a safety training coverage problem rather than a fundamental alignment failure.
