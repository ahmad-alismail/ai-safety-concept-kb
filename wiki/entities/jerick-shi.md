# Jerick Shi

## Profile
Researcher at Carnegie Mellon University working on AI safety and LLM deception. First author of a unified three-dimensional taxonomy of LLM deception spanning hallucination to scheming.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/shi-et-al-2026]] — First author of "From Hallucination to Scheming: A Unified Taxonomy and Benchmark Analysis for LLM Deception"

## Conceptual Framework
With co-authors, argues that behavioral and strategic deception should be unified under a single dimensional framework rather than treated as categorically different phenomena. The distinction is framed as an empirical question about the causal origin of deceptive outputs (training dynamics vs. goal-directed optimization), resolvable via interpretability methods.
