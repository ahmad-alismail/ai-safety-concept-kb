# Paul Christiano

## Profile
Founder and head of the Alignment Research Center (ARC). Former researcher at OpenAI. Pioneer in AI alignment research, particularly in RLHF and iterated amplification. Co-author of "Model Evaluation for Extreme Risks" (Shevlane et al. 2023).

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/shevlane-et-al-2023]] — co-author; brings alignment research perspective to evaluation framework
- [[sources/kinniment-et-al-2023]] — co-author; ARA evaluation of LM agents at METR (formerly ARC Evals)
- ARC Evals — led development of self-proliferation evaluations referenced as early exemplar work in Shevlane et al. (2023) and used to evaluate GPT-4 and Claude pre-deployment
