# Vojtěch Kovařík

## Profile
Researcher at the Department of Computer Science, Czech Technical University in Prague. Works on game-theoretic analysis of AI evaluation and safety. Received funding from Cooperative AI Foundation, Czech Science Foundation, and the Long-Term Future Fund.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/kovarik-et-al-2025]] — First author of "AI Testing Should Account for Sophisticated Strategic Behaviour" — position paper applying game theory to formalise and scrutinise evaluation-based safety cases
- Kovařík, Oesterheld & Conitzer (2023) — "Game Theory with Simulation of Other Players" (IJCAI)
- Kovařík, Oesterheld & Conitzer (2024) — "Recursive Joint Simulation in Games"
- Kovařík, Sauerberg, Hammond & Conitzer (2024) — "Game Theory with Simulation in the Presence of Unpredictable Randomisation"
