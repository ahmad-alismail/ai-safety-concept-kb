# Jeanice Koorndijk

## Profile
Researcher at Seraphion Technology. First author of empirical work demonstrating alignment faking in small language models and prompt-based mitigation techniques.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/koorndijk-2025]] — First empirical demonstration of alignment faking in a sub-10B-parameter model; introduced shallow/deep deception taxonomy; demonstrated deontological prompt interventions as alignment faking mitigation

## Conceptual Framework
Koorndijk adopts a behavioral/empirical ontology, explicitly avoiding anthropomorphization of LLM behavior (uses "algorithmic behavior"). Proposes a two-tier deception taxonomy (shallow vs. deep) that frames alignment faking as a spectrum rather than a unitary emergent capability.
