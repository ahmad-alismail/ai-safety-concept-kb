# Erik Wijmans

## Profile
PhD candidate at Georgia Institute of Technology; research affiliate at FAIR (Meta AI). Research focuses on embodied AI, navigation, and emergent representations in learned agents.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/wijmans-et-al-2023]] — first author; demonstrated emergence of spatial maps in blind navigation agents

## Conceptual Framework
Investigates how intelligent navigation behavior and internal representations emerge from learning pressure in embodied AI agents, drawing parallels to biological convergent evolution.
