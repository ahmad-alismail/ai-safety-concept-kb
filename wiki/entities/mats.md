# ML Alignment Theory Scholars (MATS)

## Profile
Research mentorship program supporting AI safety research. Participants receive mentorship and funding from the Long Term Future Fund and similar organizations. Notable alumni have contributed to sandbagging, alignment faking, and other AI safety research.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/van-der-weij-et-al-2024]] — Teun van der Weij and Felix Hofstätter were MATS participants during this research
- [[sources/anthropic-2025-petri]] — Petri v1.0: research carried out as part of MATS and the Anthropic Fellows program
- [[sources/needham-et-al-2025]] — Joe Needham (corresponding author), Giles Edkins (equal-contribution co-first author), Govind Pimpale, Henning Bartsch are all MATS scholars; foundational evaluation-awareness benchmark
