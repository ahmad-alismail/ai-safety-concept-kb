# Apart Research

## Profile
Independent AI safety research organization running collaborative fellowships and sprints. Hosted the multi-institutional collaboration behind the noise injection sandbagging detection paper, with co-authors drawn from Apart fellows and external research labs including Anthropic, Apollo Research, SecureBio, TU Munich, Tufts, and Geodesic Research.
