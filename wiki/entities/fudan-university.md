# Fudan University

## Profile
Major Chinese research university in Shanghai. The School of Computer Science / Computation and Artificial Intelligence Innovative College conducts research on AI safety evaluation, evaluation faking, and end-to-end agentic capability assessment. Group is led by Min Yang (corresponding author on multiple Fudan AI-safety papers).

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/pan-et-al-2025]] — first empirical demonstration that 11/32 LLM-powered AI systems already cross the self-replication red line (end-to-end)
- [[sources/fan-et-al-2026]] — first systematic study of evaluation faking in AI safety evaluations
