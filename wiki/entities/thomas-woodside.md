# Thomas Woodside

## Profile
Researcher at the [[entities/center-for-ai-safety]]. Co-author of the Hendrycks et al. 2023 overview of catastrophic AI risks; also co-author of "Artificial Influence: An Analysis Of AI-Driven Persuasion" (Burtell & Woodside 2023) and "Examples of AI Improving AI" (2023, web resource). Focus on AI-driven persuasion, automated AI R&D, and governance implications of race dynamics.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/hendrycks-et-al-2023]] — co-author of "An Overview of Catastrophic AI Risks"
- Burtell & Woodside 2023 — "Artificial Influence: An Analysis Of AI-Driven Persuasion" (arXiv:2303.08721)
- Woodside et al. 2023 — "Examples of AI Improving AI" (ai-improving-ai.safe.ai)
- [[sources/pan-et-al-2023]] — co-author of "Do the Rewards Justify the Means? Measuring Trade-Offs Between Rewards and Ethical Behavior in the MACHIAVELLI Benchmark" (ICML 2023).
