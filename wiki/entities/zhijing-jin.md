# Zhijing Jin

## Profile
Senior researcher leading Jinesis Lab at the University of Toronto & Vector Institute, with affiliations at the Max Planck Institute and EuroSafeAI. Works on AI safety, causal reasoning, and LLM deception.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/shi-et-al-2026]] — Equal supervisor (with Vincent Conitzer) of "From Hallucination to Scheming: A Unified Taxonomy and Benchmark Analysis for LLM Deception"

## Conceptual Framework
Equal supervisor on the Shi et al. deception taxonomy work, which argues for a dimensional rather than categorical view of LLM deception and identifies pragmatic distortion as a critically under-covered mechanism in existing benchmarks.
