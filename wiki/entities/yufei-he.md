# Yufei He

## Profile
PhD researcher at the National University of Singapore (NUS). Works on AI safety, alignment evaluation, and graph representation learning. First author of the InstrumentalEval benchmark for measuring instrumental convergence behaviors in LLMs.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/he-et-al-2025]] — first author; introduced InstrumentalEval benchmark for evaluating instrumental convergence in RL-trained vs. RLHF-trained LLMs

## Conceptual Framework
Treats instrumental convergence as an observable behavioral tendency measurable through structured benchmark scenarios. Proposes a flat taxonomy of six behavioral categories as parallel manifestations of the same underlying phenomenon. Emphasizes the role of training methodology (RL vs. RLHF) as the primary determinant of instrumental convergence propensity.
