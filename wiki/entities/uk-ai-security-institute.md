# UK AI Security Institute (AISI)

## Profile
UK government organization responsible for AI safety evaluation. Develops evaluation frameworks (Inspect) and conducts pre-deployment assessments of frontier AI models. Formerly known as the UK AI Safety Institute.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/black-et-al-2025]] — RepliBench autonomous replication benchmark
- Inspect evaluation framework (used across multiple AI safety evaluations)
- Pre-deployment evaluations of OpenAI o1 and other frontier models
