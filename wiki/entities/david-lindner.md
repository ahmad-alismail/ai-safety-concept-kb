# David Lindner

## Profile
Researcher at Google DeepMind. Co-author of the MISR benchmark for evaluating instrumental self-reasoning. Also contributed to Phuong et al. (2024) on dangerous capability evaluations (where self-reasoning was one of five evaluation domains).

## Conceptual Framework
Part of the DeepMind team that originally defined self-reasoning as a dangerous capability domain. Co-developed the expanded MISR framework extending self-reasoning evaluations beyond self-modification to five categories.
