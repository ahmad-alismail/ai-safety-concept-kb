# Mia Hopman

## Profile
Researcher at LASR Labs. Co-first author (with Jannes Elstner) of the first systematic study of scheming propensity in LLM agents.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/hopman-et-al-2026]] (co-first author)

## Conceptual Framework
Developed the scheming incentive framework decomposing scheming into agent factors (goal-directedness, agency) and environmental factors (stakes, oversight, outcome influence). Emphasizes propensity over capability for deployment risk assessment.
