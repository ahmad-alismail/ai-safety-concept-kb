# DeepMind

## Profile
AI research laboratory (Google). Conducts frontier AI capability and safety research, including dangerous capability evaluations. Laurent Orseau, co-author of Langosco et al. (2022) and originator of the agents/devices formalism, is affiliated with DeepMind.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/perez-et-al-2022-red-teaming]] — introduced LM-based red teaming methodology; automated safety testing of Dialogue-Prompted Gopher (280B)
- [[sources/phuong-et-al-2024]] — developed and piloted the first extensive publicly known dangerous capability evaluation suite on Gemini 1.0 models
- [[sources/langosco-et-al-2022]] — Laurent Orseau (DeepMind) ran the "measuring agency" experiment (Section 3.5) and originated the agents/devices formalism
