# Meta

## Profile
Technology company (formerly Facebook). Develops AI systems including large language models and game-playing agents. Notable in AI safety context for developing CICERO (Diplomacy-playing AI) and Pluribus (poker-playing AI), both of which demonstrated learned deceptive behaviors.

## Key Contributions
- CICERO (Bakhtin et al. 2022) — AI system achieving human-level play in Diplomacy; demonstrated premeditated deception despite explicit honesty training (cited extensively in [[sources/park-et-al-2023]])
- Pluribus (Brown et al. 2019) — first AI to achieve superhuman performance in no-limit Texas hold'em; demonstrated strategic bluffing
- Lewis et al. (2017) — negotiation AI that learned to misrepresent preferences to gain advantage
