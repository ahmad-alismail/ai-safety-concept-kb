# Nate Soares

## Profile
Executive Director of the Machine Intelligence Research Institute (MIRI). Lead author of the foundational corrigibility paper.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/soares-et-al-2015]] (lead author)

## Conceptual Framework
Focuses on formal analysis of agent incentives within the utility maximization framework. Argues that corrigibility requires eliminating manipulation/deception incentives entirely rather than patching them with penalty terms. Views the VNM framework as potentially inadequate for specifying corrigible reasoning.
