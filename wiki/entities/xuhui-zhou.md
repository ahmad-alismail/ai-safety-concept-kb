# Xuhui Zhou

## Profile

Researcher at Carnegie Mellon University. Creator of the Sotopia framework for social intelligence evaluation and the Haicosystem framework for sandboxing safety risks in human-AI interactions.

## Key Contributions

- [[sources/vijayvargiya-et-al-2025]] — co-author of OPENAGENTSAFETY framework
- Sotopia framework (Zhou et al. 2024b) — interactive evaluation for social intelligence in language agents
- Haicosystem (Zhou et al. 2024a) — ecosystem for sandboxing safety risks in human-AI interactions
