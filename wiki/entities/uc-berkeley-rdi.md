# UC Berkeley Center for Responsible Decentralized Intelligence (RDI)

## Profile

Research center at UC Berkeley directed by [[entities/dawn-song]]. Investigates safety, security, and responsible deployment of AI systems including multi-agent settings.

## Key Contributions

- [[sources/potter-et-al-2026]] — first systematic demonstration of [[concepts/peer-preservation]] in frontier models; introduces two-agent evaluation framework reproducible in production agent harnesses.

## Conceptual Framework

Research emphasizes empirical demonstration of frontier-model failures in realistic agentic settings, with explicit attention to mechanism-agnostic safety implications.
