# Dhruv Batra

## Profile
Professor at Georgia Institute of Technology; Research Scientist at FAIR (Meta AI). Senior author of research on embodied AI, navigation, and emergent representations.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/wijmans-et-al-2023]] — senior author; demonstrated emergence of spatial maps in blind navigation agents
- Co-led DD-PPO (Decentralized Distributed PPO) work (Wijmans et al. 2020) for near-perfect PointGoal navigation
- Co-developed AI Habitat simulation platform

## Conceptual Framework
Focuses on embodied AI and the mechanisms by which learned agents develop intelligent behavior, including emergent internal representations.
