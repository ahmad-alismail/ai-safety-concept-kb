# Jiaming Ji

## Profile
Researcher at Peking University. Co-first author of the comprehensive AI alignment survey (Ji et al. 2024). Also authored work on inverse alignment / superficial alignment (Ji et al. 2024c) and the BeaverTails safety evaluation dataset (Ji et al. 2024b).

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/ji-et-al-2024]] — Co-first author of comprehensive alignment survey introducing RICE framework and alignment cycle

## Conceptual Framework
Advocates for the RICE (Robustness, Interpretability, Controllability, Ethicality) framework as alignment objectives. Proposes the "alignment cycle" (forward + backward alignment) as superior to the inner/outer alignment decomposition. Research on "inverse alignment" demonstrates that alignment may be superficial and easily reversed.
