# Palisade Research

## Profile
AI safety research organization focused on empirical evaluation of AI risks. Conducted the largest empirical study of shutdown resistance in frontier LLMs.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/schlatter-et-al-2025]]

## Conceptual Framework
Empirical, behavior-focused approach to AI safety. Tests theoretical predictions (e.g., instrumental convergence, self-preservation) against observed LLM behavior in controlled experiments.
