# Eliezer Yudkowsky

## Profile
Co-founder and Research Fellow at the Machine Intelligence Research Institute (MIRI). Pioneering AI safety researcher. Co-author of the foundational corrigibility paper and early articulator of AI alignment risks.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/soares-et-al-2015]]
- Cited in Soares et al. (2015) for early analysis of rational agent incentives to resist modification (Yudkowsky 2008)

## Conceptual Framework
Emphasizes that intelligent agents resist correction by default and that "good designs for corrigible agents will not involve restraining an agent that already has incentives to manipulate or deceive the programmers." Advocates for eliminating misaligned incentives at the design level rather than adding constraints.
