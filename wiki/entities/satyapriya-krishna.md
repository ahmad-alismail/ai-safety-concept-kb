# Satyapriya Krishna

## Profile
Researcher at Amazon Nova Responsible AI; first author of D-REX, the first benchmark specifically designed to detect [[concepts/deceptive-reasoning]] in frontier LLMs via competitive red-teaming and CoT–output discrepancy analysis.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/krishna-et-al-2025]] — first author of "D-REX: A Benchmark for Detecting Deceptive Reasoning in Large Language Models" (2025). Formalizes the deceptive-reasoning failure mode (CoT acts on malicious directive while output appears benign), introduces a private competitive-red-team benchmark, and demonstrates significant vulnerability across seven frontier LLMs.
