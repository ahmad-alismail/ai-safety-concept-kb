# Xudong Pan

## Profile
Researcher at Fudan University, School of Computer Science. Co-first author (equal contribution) of the end-to-end self-replication evaluation that for the first time empirically demonstrates 11/32 LLM-powered AI systems already cross the self-replication red line. Co-author on the same Fudan group's evaluation-faking work.

**Note**: distinct from [[entities/alexander-pan]] (UC Berkeley, MACHIAVELLI benchmark).

- [[sources/fan-et-al-2026]] (co-author) — evaluation faking observer-effect study
