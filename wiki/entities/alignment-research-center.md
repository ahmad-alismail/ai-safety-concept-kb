# Alignment Research Center (ARC)

## Profile
AI safety research organization founded by [[entities/paul-christiano]]. Focused on alignment research and safety evaluation. ARC Evals (the evaluations team) builds evaluations measuring dangerous capabilities, notably self-proliferation.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/kinniment-et-al-2023]] — ARC Evals (later [[entities/metr|METR]]) evaluated 4 LM agents on 12 ARA-relevant tasks; first systematic autonomous replication evaluation
- ARC Evals ran self-proliferation evaluations on GPT-4 and Claude before wider release — referenced as early exemplar work in [[sources/shevlane-et-al-2023]]
- Evaluated GPT-4 pre-deployment (cited in OpenAI 2023a)
