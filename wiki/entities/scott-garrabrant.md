# Scott Garrabrant

## Profile
AI safety researcher at MIRI (Machine Intelligence Research Institute). Co-author of the foundational mesa-optimization paper.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/hubinger-et-al-2019]] — Co-author of "Risks from Learned Optimization in Advanced Machine Learning Systems"
- Co-authored "Categorizing Variants of Goodhart's Law" (Manheim and Garrabrant 2018), which introduced the concept of "adversarial Goodhart" — closely related to deceptive alignment

## Conceptual Framework
Contributed to the mesa-optimization framework that decomposed alignment into inner and outer alignment problems. The adversarial Goodhart concept from his earlier work with Manheim provides a complementary framing: deceptive alignment is an instance of adversarial Goodhart where the mesa-optimizer is the agent with different goals and the base optimizer is the regulator.
