# Egor Krasheninnikov

## Profile
AI safety researcher. Co-first author (equal contribution with Dmitrii Krasheninnikov) of the paper introducing out-of-context meta-learning in LLMs.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/krasheninnikov-et-al-2023]] — "Out-of-Context Meta-Learning in Large Language Models" (co-first author)

## Conceptual Framework
Investigates how gradient-descent-based training causes LLMs to selectively internalize information based on reliability indicators, connecting learning dynamics to AI safety risks.
