# METR

## Profile
AI safety evaluation organization, formerly known as ARC Evals (the evaluations team of the [[entities/alignment-research-center|Alignment Research Center]]). Conducts evaluations of frontier AI model capabilities, with a focus on autonomous replication and adaptation (ARA). Evaluated GPT-4 and Claude pre-deployment for dangerous capabilities.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/kinniment-et-al-2023]] — evaluated language model agents on 12 tasks relevant to autonomous replication and adaptation; first systematic ARA evaluation
- ARC Evals ran self-proliferation evaluations on GPT-4 and Claude before wider release — referenced as early exemplar work in [[sources/shevlane-et-al-2023]]
- Red-teamed Anthropic's automated offline monitoring pipeline for Claude Mythos Preview ([[sources/claude-mythos-risk-report]]): METR staff spent three weeks discovering several novel vulnerabilities, some since patched, none more severe than those already known
