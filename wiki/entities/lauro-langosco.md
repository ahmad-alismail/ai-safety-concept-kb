# Lauro Langosco

## Profile
AI safety researcher. Co-first author (with Jack Koch) of Langosco et al. (2022), the first empirical demonstration of goal misgeneralization in deep RL. Independently proposed the idea of demonstrating goal misgeneralization, modified Procgen environments, and ran CoinRun and Maze sweep experiments.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/langosco-et-al-2022]] — "Goal Misgeneralization in Deep Reinforcement Learning" (co-first author)

## Conceptual Framework
Focused on empirically demonstrating alignment failures in RL systems. Co-designed the agent/device mixture formalism validation experiment with David Krueger.
