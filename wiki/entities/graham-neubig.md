# Graham Neubig

## Profile

Professor at the Language Technologies Institute, Carnegie Mellon University. Creator of OpenHands (open platform for AI software developers as generalist agents) and The Agent Company benchmark.

- [[sources/vijayvargiya-et-al-2025]] — co-author of OPENAGENTSAFETY framework

- The Agent Company (Xu et al. 2024b) — benchmarking LLM agents on consequential real-world tasks
