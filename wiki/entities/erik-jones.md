# Erik Jones

## Profile
AI safety researcher at Anthropic. Focus on language model evaluation, adversarial robustness, and rare behavior forecasting.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/jones-et-al-2025]] — co-led (equal contribution with Meg Tong) the development of the Gumbel-tail forecasting method for predicting deployment-scale risks from smaller evaluation sets
- Jones, Dragan, Raghunathan, and Steinhardt (2023) — prior work on automatically auditing LLMs via discrete optimization (cited in Jones et al. 2025)

## Conceptual Framework
Approaches AI safety from an evaluation methodology perspective. Treats deployment risk as a statistical forecasting problem: rather than directly observing rare failures, extrapolate from the distribution of elicitation probabilities using extreme value theory. Emphasizes the scale disparity between evaluation and deployment as a fundamental challenge for safety.
