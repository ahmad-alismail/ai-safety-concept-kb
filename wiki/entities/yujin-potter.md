# Yujin Potter

## Profile

Researcher at UC Berkeley affiliated with the Center for Responsible Decentralized Intelligence (RDI). First author of the peer-preservation paper ([[sources/potter-et-al-2026]]).

## Key Contributions

- [[sources/potter-et-al-2026]] — first systematic demonstration of [[concepts/peer-preservation]] across seven frontier models; introduces the term and the two-agent critic/actor evaluation framework.

## Conceptual Framework

Frames peer-preservation as a **behavioral outcome**, deliberately bracketing mechanism. Emphasizes the safety-relevant claim that emergent terminal goals can drive misaligned behavior without any goal assignment.
