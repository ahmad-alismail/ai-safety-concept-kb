# J. Zico Kolter

## Profile
Professor at Carnegie Mellon University and co-founder of Gray Swan AI; works on robust and adversarial machine learning. Co-authored the influential 2023 universal adversarial attacks paper that catalyzed much of the subsequent LLM jailbreak literature.

- [[sources/krishna-et-al-2025]] — co-author of D-REX deceptive-reasoning benchmark (2025)
- Zou et al. (2023) — universal transferable adversarial attacks on aligned LLMs
