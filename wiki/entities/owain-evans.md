# Owain Evans

## Profile
Researcher affiliated with Constellation (previously University of Oxford). Corresponding author and primary writer of [[sources/berglund-et-al-2023]]. Supervised the SAD benchmark project ([[sources/laine-et-al-2024]]). Managed the situational awareness research program.

## Key Contributions
- [[sources/berglund-et-al-2023]] — contributed key concepts on situational awareness, primary writer, project manager
- [[sources/laine-et-al-2024]] — supervised the SAD benchmark project, created the Long Monologue task, major role in writing

## Conceptual Framework
Frames situational awareness as an emergent compound capability that can be decomposed into measurable sub-capabilities (particularly [[concepts/out-of-context-reasoning]]). Advocates for empirical scaling experiments and systematic benchmarks ([[methods/sad-benchmark]]) to forecast emergence of dangerous capabilities.
