# AI Awareness

## Definitions
- **[[sources/li-et-al-2025]]**: "a system's functional ability to represent and reason about its own identity, capabilities, and informational states" — explicitly distinguished from consciousness: "the concept of AI awareness defined as a system's functional ability to represent and reason about its own identity, capabilities, and informational states — has emerged as an important and tractable research frontier" (§1). — classified as: **functional capacity** (umbrella meta-construct)

## Classification
Level of analysis: **compound meta-construct** — an umbrella category encompassing four sub-dimensions: [[concepts/meta-cognition]], [[concepts/self-awareness-ai]], [[concepts/social-awareness]], [[concepts/situational-awareness]]

## Relationships
- Decomposes into: [[concepts/meta-cognition]], [[concepts/self-awareness-ai]], [[concepts/social-awareness]], [[concepts/situational-awareness]]
- Meta-cognition positioned as "meta" layer giving rise to the other three (Fig. 1, Li et al. 2025)
- Enhances: reasoning capabilities, autonomous planning, safety and trustworthiness
- Enables risk of: [[concepts/deception]], [[concepts/alignment-faking]], [[concepts/sandbagging]], [[concepts/false-anthropomorphism]]
- Distinguished from: AI consciousness (phenomenological, scientifically unresolved "hard problem")
- Related to: [[concepts/dangerous-capabilities]] (awareness capabilities can become dangerous when combined with misalignment)

## Evidence Summary
- **[[sources/li-et-al-2025]]**: Review of evaluation literature finds that LLMs are "the first class of artificial agents empirically demonstrated, under controlled conditions, to exhibit all four key dimensions" of awareness (Table 2). Meta-cognition and situational awareness are more sophisticated than self-awareness and social awareness in current models. Awareness capabilities may emerge suddenly at critical thresholds rather than scaling linearly.

## Open Questions
- How much awareness is beneficial vs. dangerous? The paper notes "certain kinds of awareness are good (e.g., awareness of incompetence, which yields humility) while others are risky (e.g., awareness of how to deceive)" — but no clear boundary exists (§5.4)
- Can awareness sub-dimensions be independently controlled, or do they emerge together?
